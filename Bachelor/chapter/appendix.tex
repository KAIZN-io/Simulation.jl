			$R_{refdt}= \frac{phi\cdot R_{ref}\cdot r }{2\cdot d} \cdot max(\pi_t- \pi_c, 0)$\\

			$r_{bdt} = 0.2\cdot R_{refdt}$\\
			$r_{dt} = r_{osdt} + r_{bdt}$  \\

			$\frac{d}{dt}  r = r_{dt}$\\
			$\frac{d}{dt} r_{os} = r_{osdt}$\\
